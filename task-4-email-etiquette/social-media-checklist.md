# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO's

1. Keep your profile professional — use a clear name and appropriate profile photo.
2. Share academic achievements, projects, and certifications to build your portfolio.
3. Follow industry professionals, companies, and thought leaders in your field.
4. Think before you post — ask yourself if your content is appropriate for employers to see.
5. Use privacy settings wisely — control who can see your personal information.
6. Give credit when sharing others' work or ideas.
7. Engage positively — like, comment, and share content that adds value.

## DON'Ts

1. Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal financial details on any platform.
2. Do not post offensive, discriminatory, or politically inflammatory content.
3. Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information.
4. Do not accept friend/follow requests from unknown people without verifying.
5. Never post your college ID, address, or daily routine publicly.
6. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
7. Avoid posting during exams or important deadlines — stay focused.
