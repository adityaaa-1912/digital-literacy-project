# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

### General Online Safety

1. Never click on links received via WhatsApp, SMS, or email from unknown senders.
2. Always verify the URL of any website before entering personal details —
   look for https:// and the correct domain name.
3. Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor
   authentication (2FA) wherever available.
4. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or password with anyone —
   not even someone claiming to be from your bank.
5. Regularly update your apps and operating system to patch security
   vulnerabilities.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or any sensitive transactions.

### Financial / UPI Safety

7. Never enter your UPI PIN to RECEIVE money — PIN is only needed to SEND.
8. Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers — scammers use this to
   withdraw money from your account.

### Social Media Safety

9. Keep your personal information (address, phone number, daily routine)
   private on social media.
10. Do not accept friend requests from unknown people without verifying
    their identity.

### If You Are a Victim

- Immediately report at: cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
- Block your UPI / bank account by calling your bank immediately
- Take screenshots of all evidence before reporting
